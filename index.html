III · THE MASTERS

Fra Angelico gave divinity weight.

Filippo Lippi painted with a quiet, human tenderness.

Donatello freed sculpture from the wall.

Della Robbia invented a new colour for clay.

Mino da Fiesole carved a man's mind.

Botticelli's line dreamed before it described.

Leonardo da Vinci could not stop looking.

Filippino Lippi inherited his father's tenderness, and added music.
